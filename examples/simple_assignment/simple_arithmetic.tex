\textbf{Simple Arithmetic.} Solve the following arithmetic problems:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[a.] What is \(12 \times 3\)?
\item[b.] What is \(45 \div 5\)?
\item[c.] What is \(7 + 8\)?
\end{enumerate}
\ifshowsolutions
\Solutionheader\\
The answers are:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[a.] \(36\)
\item[b.] \(9\)
\item[c.] \(15\)
\end{enumerate}
\fi
